Top 3 Filipino Movies

Heneral Luna

2015

Proudly showcasing Philippine cinema | Created with love for Filipino films A historical biopic about General Antonio Luna, one of the fiercest generals of the Philippine Revolution. The film depicts his leadership of the Philippine Revolutionary Army during the Philippine-American War, showcasing his fiery temper and uncompromising patriotism.

100/100

Four Sisters and a Wedding

2013

A heartwarming family comedy-drama about four sisters who reunite when their younger brother announces his plans to get married. The film explores family dynamics, sibling rivalries, and unconditional love in a distinctly Filipino context.

8.7/10

Kita Kita

2017

A romantic comedy about a Filipino tour guide in Japan who temporarily loses her eyesight and develops a relationship with her neighbor who helps her navigate her new condition. The film became a surprise box office hit, praised for its simple yet touching story.

9.0/10
