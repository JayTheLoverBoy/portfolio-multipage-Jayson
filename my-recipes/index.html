My Recipes: Pork Adobo

Photo Gallery

Ingredients

  • 0.5 kg Pork Liempo (pork belly)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot, combine pork, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves.
  2. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for best flavor).
  3. Bring to a boil, then add water. Simmer on low heat for about 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until pork is tender.
  4. Adjust seasoning with salt if needed. Reduce the sauce to your preferred consistency.
  5. Serve hot with rice and enjoy the bold flavors!
